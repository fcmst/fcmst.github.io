Link Is CUTE

Link is the #1 video game cutie and that is a fact.

Honestly, I would love to give Nintendo an award for the cutest video game character of all time. I mean, who wouldn't? His feminine looks, his hair, his adventurous attitude... he's just so perfect! And that's not even the only thing I love about him. His various suits just add the top-tier icing onto the already gourmet cake. His stealth armor and the Gerudo set are quite possibly the best articles of clothing I have ever seen a video game character wear. And to think that he's a Nintendo character is even more astonishing.
